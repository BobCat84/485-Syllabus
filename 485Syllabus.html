Course Description

This course provides an experiential experience in innovation and entrepreneurship. It will teach you specific methods to assess and understand the industry, customers, and competitors for a new venture. You will then learn how to translate those insights into a winning venture idea, a business model, and set of distinctive new products and services.

This Course is Ideal For:

  • Future Entrepreneurs: Students who wish to start their own venture, today or in the future
  • Corporate Innovators: Students who want to create new businesses within existing corporations
  • Social Impact Leaders: Those interested in non-profits or NGOs who want to develop customer-centric solutions

Course Structure

Part 1: Ideation

  • Industry and stakeholder analysis
  • Creating venture concepts based on customer needs
  • Designing sustainable business models
  • Developing product and service strategies
  • Competitive positioning

Part 2: Implementation

  • Customer validation and reality checks
  • Identifying the right investors
  • Creating realistic financial projections
  • Developing compelling venture proposals
  • Creating and filming winning pitches

Team-Based Learning: You'll work in small teams of four to five individuals to develop and assess your own startup, corporate spin-off, or internal venture ideas.

Learning Outcomes

🧠 Expand Knowledge

Gain perspective on basic aspects of innovation, ideation, entrepreneurship, and successful business models.

🚀 Apply Ideas

Apply fundamental principles of innovation and integrate interdisciplinary coursework to your team's innovative idea.

🔍 Analyze Critically

Integrate and apply models of innovation, strategy, problem solving, and critical thinking to assess feasibility and value propositions.

📋 Synthesize

Compile a comprehensive business questionnaire and short film pitch to synthesize knowledge, analysis, and application.

🌍 Global Connections

Examine innovation and entrepreneurship globally, understanding international interdependence and opportunities.
